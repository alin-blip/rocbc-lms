# Complaints & Appeals Policy — ROCBC

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective from:** [date]

## 1. Purpose

To provide a clear, fair, and timely process for learners to raise complaints or appeal assessment decisions.

## 2. Scope

This policy covers:
- Complaints about service, support, or delivery
- Appeals against assessment decisions
- Academic misconduct decisions

## 3. Stage 1 — Informal Resolution

- Learner raises issue with tutor/assessor directly
- Response within 5 working days
- Most issues resolved at this stage

## 4. Stage 2 — Formal Complaint

- Submitted via support ticket marked "Complaint"
- Acknowledged within 48 hours
- Investigated by Centre Manager
- Response within 15 working days

## 5. Stage 3 — Assessment Appeal

- Must be submitted within 14 calendar days of receiving the assessment decision
- Grounds: procedural error, bias, new evidence, marking error
- Reviewed by IV who was not involved in original assessment
- Decision within 15 working days

## 6. Stage 4 — Independent Review

- If unsatisfied, learner may escalate to Pearson's formal complaints procedure
- ROCBC provides full evidence pack to Pearson on request

## 7. Appeals Committee

For complex cases, ROCBC convenes an Appeals Committee comprising:
- Centre Manager
- Internal Verifier (independent of the case)
- External Pearson representative (where required)

## 8. Records

All complaints and appeals are documented and retained for 5 years.

## 9. No Detriment

Learners will not face any disadvantage for raising a complaint or appeal in good faith.
