# Assessment Policy — ROCBC

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective from:** [date]  
**Approved by:** Centre Manager  
**Review cycle:** Annual

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the principles, procedures, and responsibilities for assessment of all Pearson qualifications delivered via distance learning at ROCBC.

## 2. Scope

Applies to all assessors, internal verifiers, learners, and Pearson-approved programmes delivered at ROCBC.

## 3. Principles

- All assessments are valid, reliable, fair, and consistent with Pearson standards
- Assessment criteria are clearly defined and shared with learners before assessment
- All assessors are occupationally competent and qualified per Pearson requirements
- Feedback is constructive, timely, and supports learner progression
- Internal verification samples a minimum of 25% of all assessment decisions

## 4. Assessment Methods

ROCBC uses the following assessment methods for distance learning:

a) **Written assignments** — submitted via LMS with authenticity declaration
b) **Video presentations** — learner records and uploads a presentation
c) **Oral questioning** — conducted via Zoom, recorded and stored
d) **Portfolio of evidence** — compiled and submitted digitally
e) **Practical observations** — live via Zoom (recorded)

## 5. Submission Rules

- All submissions must include a signed authenticity declaration
- Submissions without declaration will not be accepted
- Word count limits must be respected (±10% tolerance)
- Late submissions require approved extension request
- Resubmission allowed once (within 14 calendar days of feedback)

## 6. Marking & Feedback

- Assessors must provide feedback within **10 working days** of submission
- Feedback must reference specific assessment criteria
- Grades: Pass / Merit / Distinction / Refer / Fail
- All Refer/Fail/Resubmit decisions are flagged for Internal Verification

## 7. Internal Verification

- 100% of new assessor's first 3 submissions are IV sampled
- 25% random sample for established assessors
- 100% of all Pass/Merit/Distinction decisions are IV sampled
- 100% of all Refer/Fail decisions are IV sampled
- IV must complete review within 5 working days

## 8. Plagiarism & AI

- All submissions are screened with Turnitin/Copyleaks
- AI-generated content must be declared by the learner
- See Plagiarism & AI Policy for full details

## 9. Records

- All assessment records are retained for a minimum of **5 years**
- Evidence vault stores: submissions, feedback, IV records, plagiarism reports

## 10. Appeals

Learners may appeal assessment decisions within 14 days. See Complaints Policy.
