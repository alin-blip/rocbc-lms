# Internal Verification Policy — ROCBC

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective from:** [date]

## 1. Purpose

To ensure consistency, fairness, and accuracy of all assessment decisions across Pearson programmes.

## 2. IV Personnel

ROCBC appoints a qualified Internal Verifier who is:
- Occupationallly competent in the subject area
- Trained in Pearson IV requirements
- Not an assessor on the same programme (to maintain independence)

## 3. Sampling Strategy

| Category | Sample Rate |
|----------|------------|
| New assessor — first 3 submissions | **100%** |
| New assignment — first 3 submissions | **100%** |
| Routine (established assessor) | **25% random** |
| Pass/Merit/Distinction decisions | **100%** |
| Refer/Fail decisions | **100%** |
| Learner complaints about assessment | **100%** |

## 4. IV Process

1. Selection of sample (random or targeted)
2. Review of: assignment brief, learner submission, assessor feedback, plagiarism report
3. IV decision:
   - **Approved** — assessment stands
   - **Changes required** — assessor amends and resubmits to IV
   - **Remark** — IV reassesses independently
4. Record decision in assessment_feedback record
5. Archive in evidence vault

## 5. Timeline

- IV review must be completed within **5 working days** of assessor submission
- Feedback is NOT released to learner until IV approval

## 6. Reporting

Quarterly IV reports include:
- Sample size per programme
- Agree/disagree rate per assessor
- Common issues identified
- Recommendations for improvement

## 7. Records

All IV records are retained for **5 years**.
