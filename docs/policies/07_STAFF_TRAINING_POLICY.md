# Staff Training & Development Policy — ROCBC

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective from:** [date]

## 1. Purpose

To ensure all ROCBC staff are appropriately trained and competent to deliver distance learning programmes in compliance with Pearson standards.

## 2. Onboarding Training

All new staff complete:
- Pearson qualification standards training
- Platform/LMS usage training
- Assessment and feedback training
- Plagiarism and AI detection training
- Safeguarding and GDPR training
- Equality and diversity training

## 3. Role-Specific Training

### Assessors (Teachers)
- Understanding Pearson assessment criteria
- Marking and grading procedures
- Feedback best practices
- Oral questioning techniques
- Plagiarism detection tool usage

### Internal Verifiers
- Sampling methodology
- IV decision-making
- Quality assurance processes
- Audit preparation

### Admin
- Learner verification procedures
- Data management
- Support protocols

## 4. Continuing Professional Development

- Annual CPD requirement: minimum 30 hours per staff member
- CPD log maintained in platform
- Includes: webinars, Pearson updates, conferences, internal workshops

## 5. Performance Monitoring

- Quarterly observation of teaching/sessions
- Annual performance review
- Student feedback incorporated
- IV reports inform training needs

## 6. Records

All staff training records are maintained in the platform and retained for the duration of employment plus 3 years.
