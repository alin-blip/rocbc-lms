# Learner Support Policy — ROCBC

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective from:** [date]

## 1. Purpose

To ensure all distance learning learners receive appropriate academic, technical, and pastoral support throughout their programme.

## 2. Pre-Enrolment Support

- Detailed programme information on website
- Eligibility and entry requirements consultation
- Application guidance

## 3. Induction Support

Before accessing courses, every learner completes:
- Identity verification (ID upload + selfie)
- Platform induction (video walkthrough)
- Policy acceptance (all 8 policies)
- Induction quiz (must pass 70%)

## 4. Academic Support

- Tutor availability: weekly live Q&A sessions
- Feedback on assignments within 10 working days
- Additional 1:1 tutoring available on request
- Study skills resources on platform

## 5. Technical Support

- LMS support via ticketing system (response within 4 business hours)
- Platform troubleshooting guides
- Phone support during business hours

## 6. Pastoral Support

- Designated learner support officer
- Monthly check-in emails
- Mental health and wellbeing resources
- Signposting to external support services

## 7. Response Times

| Type | Target |
|------|--------|
| Technical support ticket | 4 hours |
| Academic query | 24 hours |
| Assessment feedback | 10 working days |
| Pastoral support | 24 hours |
| Complaint acknowledgment | 48 hours |

## 8. Communication Channels

- Platform messaging system
- Email
- Zoom (live sessions + 1:1s)
- Forum
- Phone (office hours)

## 9. Records

All support interactions are logged in the platform and retained per data retention policy.
